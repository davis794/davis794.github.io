The Solution

CardioSpark’s Residential AED Integrated Network (RAIN) makes communities safer by deploying Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) for use if a resident suffers Sudden Cardiac Arrest. 

SCA is the leading cause of death of adults over the age of 40. More than 350,000 men and women of all ages experience an SCA event every year, most of them in private homes. Tragically, 90% of out-of-hospital victims die due to lack of timely defibrillation.  

RAIN provides and maintains AED's throughout the community, trains residents, and delivers the only 911 dispatch integrated alert system, to make your community a Heart Safe Community®.

RAIN includes:

  • - Upfront planning with HOA leadership
  • - Education and awareness events for the community
  • - CPR and AED resident training
  • - Regular monitoring of device readiness
  • - Proprietary alert system integrated with local 911 CAD dispatch 

Conquering a Common Killer


The number of Sudden Cardiac Arrest fatalities is as great as the number of deaths from Alzheimer’s Disease, assault with firearms, breast cancer, cervical cancer, colorectal cancer, diabetes, HIV, house fires, motor vehicle accidents, prostate cancer and suicides combined.

The good news is that this appalling statistic can be reversed. The likelihood of survival is boosted 6- to 10-fold if defibrillation is administered within 3-5 minutes. The key to saving lives is making AEDs accessible where SCA is most likely to occur… at home.
